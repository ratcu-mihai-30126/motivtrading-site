How to use resources

Practical use

These materials are here to reduce decision fatigue. The more you standardize your preparation, execution, and review, the easier it becomes to trade with discipline.

Important rule

Professional mindset

Do not collect resources just to feel productive. Pick the ones you will actually use every week. The best resource is the one that becomes part of your process.

Checklist pack
Pre-Trade Checklist
Use before entry
A simple checklist to review context, setup quality, trigger, invalidation, and reward-to-risk before placing a trade.
What to checkMarket condition, structural bias, session timing, setup fit, and target logic.
Best useRead it before every order until the process becomes automatic.
GoalReduce impulsive trades and improve execution quality.

Review pack
Post-Trade Review Sheet
Use after exit
A review template for recording what happened, what followed the plan, and what should improve next time.
What to reviewEntry quality, stop logic, target logic, execution discipline, and emotional state.
Best useComplete it after meaningful trades or at the end of the session.
GoalBuild honest feedback loops instead of relying on memory.
