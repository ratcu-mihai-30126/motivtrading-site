Why these tools matter

Professional edge

Most traders do not fail because they cannot read a chart. They fail because they size too large, ignore daily limits, or enter trades with weak reward-to-risk. These tools remove that guesswork and bring structure to every decision.

Best practices

Use these rules
Risk firstCalculate size from invalidation, never from confidence.
Daily cutoffStop after your daily loss limit, even if you feel another trade is coming.
Think in RJudge trades in multiples of risk, not just dollars.
Consistency winsSame risk model, same process, same review language.

Homework

Build your rules
Use these tools to define your standard risk model. Write down your account risk per trade, daily loss cap, and minimum acceptable reward-to-risk. Then use those numbers for your next ten trades without changing them mid-session.
