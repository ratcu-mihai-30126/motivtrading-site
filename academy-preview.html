Preview lesson: what makes a breakout worth trading

A quality breakout is not just price moving fast. You want structure, a clear area of compression, a clean push through the level, and enough room to the next target to justify the risk. The point is not to chase movement. The point is to recognize when the market is finally giving you a valid reason to act.

Wait for price to compress around a clear level instead of forcing entries in the middle of noise.
Map the invalidation before the entry so the trade has structure, not hope.
Only take the trade when there is a logical path to the next target, not just momentum candles.
